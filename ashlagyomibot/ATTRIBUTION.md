## Fair Use & Public Domain

The original Hebrew texts of the Arizal, Baal Shem Tov, and classical Chassidic masters are in the public domain.

The writings of Baal HaSulam and RABASH are widely available for educational purposes. This project:
- Uses brief quotations for educational purposes
- Provides proper attribution and source links
- Encourages readers to explore the full original works
- Is non-commercial and open source
